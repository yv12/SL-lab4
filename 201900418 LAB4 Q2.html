A Paragraph:

Prime numbers are considered as the numbers which retain only two factors i.e. one and itself. It implies that the number can be divided only by 1 and itself. All prime numbers are an odd number apart from 2.
Composite numbers are those numbers that retain more than two factors. It implies that the number except one and itself can be divided by other numbers also. Every non- prime number is a composite number.
